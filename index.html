OTTR: Open-source Tools for Training Resources


Why OTTR?

OTTR makes publishing and maintaining courses on multiple platforms less painful!


Write 1 time ‚û°Ô∏è publish 3 times!

  • Edit and write a course in R Markdown files and have it published to multiple different platforms:
    • Bookdown
    • Leanpub
    • Coursera
  • Use automagic conversion to ease the lift of prepping the material for different platforms.

Simplify publishing steps:

  • Have Github action robots do your repetitive tasks like spell check and re-rendering.
  • If you plan to include code, use our Docker image for consistency across authors as well as to help you avoid dependency hell.

Customize for your own needs:

  • Follow our guides to customize the style of your course to show your own branding.
  • Customize your own Docker image for your particular needs.
